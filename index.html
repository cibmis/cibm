Acceleration Banking Think your money can make something of itself? We do.

Think you should receive benefits just for using your account? We do too. Visit your nearest branch to learn more.

*Annual Percentage Yield accurate as of 5/18/15. Variable rate accounts and interest rates are subject to change. Fees may reduce earnings. Accounts closed within 6 months of opening date are subject to a $25.00 closing fee. Limit one Acceleration Checking and Acceleration Savings account per primary social security number. Acceleration Checking: Minimum balance to open account $100. Customer must initiate set up of e-statements through online banking, establish a monthly direct deposit and perform 12 debit card purchases each month to qualify and receive tiered rates and ATM surcharge refunds up to $12. Transactions may take one or more business days from the date transaction was made to post and clear the account. Checking account balances of $0.00-$10,000.00 (1.51% APY); $10,000.01 and above (.25% APY). If qualifications are not met, all funds in checking account earn .05% APY. If Acceleration Savings is also opened, interest earned on Acceleration Checking is automatically transferred to Acceleration Savings and then compounds at Acceleration Savings rates. Acceleration Savings: Account can only be opened in conjunction with Acceleration Checking Account. Savings account balances of $0.00-$10,000.00 (.50% APY); $10,000.01 and above (.25% APY). If qualifications of Acceleration Checking account are not met during the month, all funds in savings account earn .05% APY.
